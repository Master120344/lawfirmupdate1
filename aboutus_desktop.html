About The Kershaw Law Firm P.C.

Dedicated to navigating the complexities of U.S. employment-based immigration, we partner with businesses nationwide to secure their vital H-2A and H-2B workforce.

Our Guiding Mission

At The Kershaw Law Firm P.C., our mission is to provide unparalleled legal guidance and strategic solutions to U.S. employers seeking to build and maintain their essential workforce through the H-2A and H-2B visa programs. We are driven by a commitment to integrity, meticulousness, and achieving successful outcomes that empower your business operations.

We understand that navigating the intricacies of U.S. immigration law can be daunting. Our firm was founded on the principle of demystifying this process, offering clear, actionable advice, and serving as a steadfast advocate for your interests from initial consultation through final adjudication and beyond.

Focused H-2A & H-2B Proficiency

The landscape of temporary work visas is constantly evolving. The Kershaw Law Firm P.C. maintains a singular focus on the H-2A (agricultural) and H-2B (non-agricultural) visa categories. This dedicated concentration allows us to cultivate a profound understanding regarding:

  • Intricate regulatory requirements from the Department of Labor (DOL), U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS), and other governing bodies.
  • Evolving policy interpretations and adjudication trends affecting petition success.
  • The critical timelines and procedural nuances essential for timely workforce deployment.
  • Proactive compliance strategies to mitigate risks and ensure long-term program viability for your business.

We do more than process applications; we partner with you to understand your unique business needs and develop comprehensive visa strategies tailored to your particular industry, whether it's agriculture, landscaping, hospitality, construction, or other sectors reliant on a dedicated seasonal or temporary workforce.
