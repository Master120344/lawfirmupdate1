Comprehensive Visa Services

Our practice is exclusively focused on H-2A and H-2B visas, allowing us to offer an unparalleled depth of knowledge and service efficiency. We manage the entire lifecycle of your visa petition:

  • Initial Consultation & Eligibility Assessment: Thorough analysis of your business needs and visa pathway viability.
  • Application Preparation & Filing: Meticulous compilation and submission of all required documentation to the DOL and USCIS.
  • Recruitment Process Guidance: Navigating and documenting mandatory U.S. worker recruitment efforts.
  • Responding to RFEs & Audits: Expert handling of Requests for Evidence, Notices of Intent to Deny, and DOL audits.
  • Consular Processing & Compliance: Assistance with worker visa interviews and ongoing employer compliance support.
