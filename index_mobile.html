Our Commitment to You

At The Kershaw Law Firm, P.C., we operate with integrity, in-depth legal knowledge, and a strong dedication to our clients. We assist U.S. businesses in managing the H-2A (agricultural) and H-2B (non-agricultural) temporary foreign worker visa programs.

Our skilled legal team carefully works through the detailed regulations from the Department of Labor (DOL) and U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS). We prepare your petitions accurately and aim for efficient processing, recognizing that timely workforce solutions are vital for your business.
