About Our Firm

Founded on principles of integrity, expertise, and client-focused service, The Kershaw Law Firm, P.C. is a leading provider of legal solutions for U.S. businesses requiring temporary foreign workers through the H-2A (agricultural) and H-2B (non-agricultural) visa programs.

Our seasoned team navigates the intricate regulations set by the Department of Labor (DOL) and U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) with precision, ensuring your petitions are prepared accurately and processed efficiently. We understand the critical need for timely workforce solutions and are committed to achieving successful outcomes for your business.
