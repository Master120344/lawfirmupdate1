The Kershaw Law Firm P.C. provides end-to-end legal services primarily focused on the H-2A Agricultural Visa and H-2B Non-Agricultural Visa programs. Our goal is to secure the essential temporary foreign workers you need while ensuring full adherence to all regulatory requirements.

H-2A Agricultural Visa Services

For employers in the agricultural sector, the H-2A program is vital for sourcing temporary or seasonal labor. We provide comprehensive support, including:

Initial Case Assessment & Strategy

Evaluating your specific agricultural needs, seasonality, and developing a tailored H-2A petition strategy.

Temporary Labor Certification (TLC)

Preparing and filing Form ETA-9142A with the Department of Labor, including prevailing wage requests and navigating recruitment requirements.

USCIS Petition Filing (Form I-129)

Meticulously preparing and filing the Petition for a Nonimmigrant Worker with USCIS after DOL certification.

Consular Processing & Port of Entry Guidance

Assisting workers with visa applications at U.S. consulates abroad and advising on port of entry procedures.

Compliance & Audit Support

Advising on ongoing H-2A program obligations, record-keeping, and responding to DOL or USCIS audits and site visits.

Extensions & Amendments

Managing extensions of stay for H-2A workers and amending petitions due to material changes in employment.

H-2B Non-Agricultural Visa Services

The H-2B program allows U.S. employers to hire foreign nationals for temporary non-agricultural jobs. Our support includes:

Temporary Need Assessment

Assisting employers in properly documenting their one-time, seasonal, peak-load, or intermittent need for H-2B workers.

Prevailing Wage Determination (PWD)

Obtaining the required PWD from the DOL to ensure compliance with wage obligations.

DOL Application & Recruitment

Filing Form ETA-9142B and guiding employers through the mandatory U.S. worker recruitment process.

USCIS Petition & Cap Management

Filing Form I-129 with USCIS and navigating the complexities of the H-2B statutory cap.

Consular Processing Support

Advising on the visa application process for workers at U.S. embassies and consulates.

Regulatory Compliance & RFE Responses

Ensuring adherence to all H-2B regulations and proficiently responding to Requests for Evidence (RFEs).
